Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create or join a ride pool?
To create or join a ride pool, navigate to the "Create/Join Ride Pool" section on your dashboard, fill in the required details such as your location, time, and vehicle preference, and submit your request.
How can I split bills with other students?
You can use the "Bill Splitting" feature to manage shared expenses. Add the expenses and the names of students involved, and the system will calculate each person's share.
Is there a way to contact my ride mates?
Yes! Once you have matched with a ride, you can use the "Chat with Riders" feature to communicate and coordinate your trip details easily.
What should I do if I encounter technical issues?
If you face any technical problems, please reach out via the support email provided on your profile page or use the "Help & Support" section to report your issues.
How do I log out of my account securely?
You can log out anytime by clicking the "Logout" button located in the navigation bar on the dashboard or any page.
